Privacy Policy

This policy explains how No Added Sugar handles personal data through this website in line with the GDPR and related privacy laws. It is written for the current site setup: a landing page, a contact form, a map embed, and a theme preference stored in the browser.

Controller

No Added Sugar
Skopje, North Macedonia
Email: contact@noaddedsugar.dev

What We Collect

We only collect data that is necessary to run the site and respond to enquiries.

  • Contact form data: email address, subject, and message content.
  • Technical preference data: your theme choice stored in localStorage.
  • Basic request data generated by infrastructure and third-party service providers such as IP address, browser metadata, and server logs.

We do not currently run advertising trackers, behavioural profiling, or analytics cookies on this site.

How We Use Data

  • To answer project enquiries and communicate before a contract is agreed.
  • To operate, secure, and troubleshoot the website.
  • To remember your theme preference on the same device.
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable.

Legal Bases

  • Pre-contract steps: where you contact us about work, products, or services.
  • Legitimate interests: to secure the site, manage communications, and keep the service functional.
  • Legal obligation: where retention or disclosure is required by law.
  • Consent: only where a specific feature requires it.

Third-Party Services

This site currently uses the following third-party services:

  • FormSubmit to process contact form submissions and forward them to our email inbox.
  • Leaflet as the map library.
  • CARTO / OpenStreetMap to load map tiles and attribution data.
  • unpkg to serve certain front-end library assets.

When those services are loaded, they may receive technical request data such as your IP address and browser headers. Their own privacy terms also apply.

Cookies And Local Storage

This site does not currently set advertising or analytics cookies. It does store a theme preference in your browser using localStorage so the site can remember light or dark mode.

Retention

We keep enquiry data only for as long as needed to respond, evaluate a project opportunity, maintain relevant business records, or comply with legal obligations. If there is no ongoing relationship, we aim to review and delete stale enquiry data within 24 months unless a longer period is required.

International Transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the EEA or your home jurisdiction. Where that happens, we expect appropriate safeguards to be used, such as standard contractual clauses or equivalent lawful transfer mechanisms.

Your Rights

Where the GDPR or similar laws apply, you may have the right to request:

  • access to your personal data,
  • correction of inaccurate data,
  • erasure of data,
  • restriction of processing,
  • data portability,
  • objection to certain processing, and
  • withdrawal of consent where processing relies on consent.

You may also have the right to complain to your local data protection authority.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. That said, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children

This site is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children through it.

Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact contact@noaddedsugar.dev.
